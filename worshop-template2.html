Strongly interacting quantum materials with robust non-local entanglement and emergent fractional excitations are opening new avenues for fundamental science and future quantum technologies. This workshop aims to bring together leading researchers in two-dimensional quantum materials and related strongly correlated systems to exchange ideas and advance the understanding of quantum matter and many-body entanglement. The program will include several sessions of invited talks from senior speakers, short talks from early-career speakers, a poster session, and a panel discussion.
